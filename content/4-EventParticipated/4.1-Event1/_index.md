# Post-Event Report: Cloud Mastery AI From Scratch

## Event Information

- **Event Name:** Cloud Mastery AI From Scratch
- **Time:** 09:00 on 14/02/2026
- **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, No. 2 Hai Trieu Street, Sai Gon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City
- **Role:** Attendee

## Main Content and Activities

- Attended the session "Automated Prompt Engineering: Enhancing LLM Output Quality".
- Learned why prompting is important and how to design effective prompts using role, instruction, context, examples, and constraints.
- Explored advanced techniques such as Chain-of-Thought, RAG, and role prompting to improve LLM output quality while optimizing cost.
- Received practical guidance on how beginners can start building AI Agents.
- Reviewed two representative projects:
	- **Locker Management (AIoT):** Integrates AWS IoT Core, Amazon Rekognition, RFID, sensors, and cameras to manage equipment borrowing and return.
	- **Plutus:** A personal finance app using Amazon Textract for smart bill OCR processing.
- Joined the Q&A session to discuss AI, Cloud, and IoT topics with speakers and students.

## Outcomes and Values Gained

- Improved understanding of Prompt Engineering and efficient LLM workflows.
- Strengthened foundational knowledge of AI Agents and AI-cloud integration on AWS.
- Developed active listening, questioning, and critical thinking skills in technical discussions.
- Learned how to connect theoretical knowledge with practical implementation through AIoT and OCR use cases.
- Built a clearer mindset on creating real products instead of only learning theory.

## Personal Experience

This event gave me a more practical perspective on learning and applying AI. The prompt engineering session helped me understand that model output quality depends heavily on how context and constraints are designed. In addition, the real project showcases motivated me to focus on building solutions with real-world value.
